Tools I use for music mixing

02/02/2019

  The church I go to I have been privileged enough to help with sound, lights, recording, and producing the services and podcasts that can be found of the Mercy Hill website. For the mixing we us a bringer x-32 compact mixing board connected to a laptop that records through a program called reaper. Reaper is also what we use for our mixing and final production of the podcast recordings we have on the website. We use amazon S3 bucket hosting to make sure downloading and streaming of the podcasts from the website is as fast and as smooth as possible.
  What I use personally for listening to, mix, and edit the services and podcasts i use a audio-technica m50x which I have recently received for christmas.
